Service Summary

Enhance an existing information security program, rebuild a struggling one, or even develop a new program for your growing business. While many security consulting and management services are available, these are the most commonly needed:

  • Virtual CISO
  • Policies & Governance
  • Risk Management
  • Asset Management
  • Vulnerability Management
  • Control Implementation

Prepare for certifications or mitigate audit findings in a way that will build lasting and valuable protections for your organization. We have experience with many regulatory requirements and industry standards such as PCI-DSS 3+, HIPAA, HITRUST, GLBA, FFIEC, NCUA, ISO 27002:2013, NIST 800-53 & Cybersecurity Framework, Center for Internet Security Critical Security Controls, and more.

Virtual CISO (vCISO)

Utilize an on-demand, industry expert to avoid the cost of bringing a full-time Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) on-board by consulting only as-needed or on a project basis.

Policies & Governance

Create clearly understood policy documents that will bring the organization in-line with compliance regulations and best security practices while directing the security program's strategy and goals.

Risk Management

Performing proper risk assessments and subsequent management of threats is one of the most commonly misunderstood and mishandled controls if not performed following the right protocol.

Asset Management

Effective security controls cannot be built to protect an environment without a complete and thorough understanding of all the systems and data that need to be protected.

Vulnerability Management

Detecting vulnerabilities in your systems before the attackers do is important, but understanding the systemic gaps from which they develop from will prevent them from materializing in the first place.

Control Implementation

Information security success requires specific information security knowledge. Relying on your risk, internal audit, or information technology departments for security expertise result in faulty controls.
