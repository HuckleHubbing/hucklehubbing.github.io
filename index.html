What We Do

Our Services

From building a security program from scratch to preparing for a formal audit, K2 InfoSec provides the expertise your organization needs at every stage.

🏗️

Program Development & Enhancement

We build, rebuild, or enhance information security programs for small- and medium-sized businesses. Whether starting from scratch or maturing an existing program, we provide the structure your organization needs.

📋

Assessments & Compliance

Prepare for, undergo, and remediate security audits based on industry standards including CMMC, NIST, PCI DSS, GLBA, and HIPAA. We guide you through every phase of the assessment process.

👔

Virtual CISO (vCISO)

Executive-level security leadership without the full-time cost. A vCISO from K2 InfoSec provides strategic direction, program oversight, and board-level communication for organizations that need leadership without a permanent hire.

📄

Policy & Governance

Create clearly understood policy documents that bring your organization in line with compliance regulations and security best practices — while directing the security program's strategy and goals.

⚖️

Risk Management

Develop a risk management process using reputable frameworks such as NIST SP 800-30/37 or ISO 27005. Understanding your organization's risk posture is the foundation of properly prioritizing future security efforts.

🔍

Audit Preparation

Facing an upcoming audit? We perform gap analyses, identify remediation priorities, and ensure your team and documentation are ready before the assessor arrives — reducing surprises and findings.

Areas of Expertise

Standards We Work In

Our team has deep, hands-on experience with the compliance frameworks that matter most to your industry.

PCI DSS

PCI DSS & QSA Assessments

As Qualified Security Assessors, we assist organizations in achieving PCI DSS compliance — from understanding applicable requirements and scope to implementing the necessary controls and conducting formal assessments.

CMMC

CMMC Compliance

Prepare for CMMC requirements through compliance support including consultations, readiness assessments, and control implementation. We help defense contractors meet their obligations under the Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification framework.

NIST

NIST Frameworks

Apply NIST Cybersecurity Framework, SP 800-53, SP 800-171, and risk management frameworks (SP 800-30/37) to build structured, defensible security programs aligned with federal and industry expectations.

HIPAA / HITRUST

Healthcare Security & HITRUST

With HITRUST CCSFP and CHQP credentials, we support healthcare organizations and their business associates in addressing HIPAA requirements and pursuing HITRUST certification.
