Assessments & Preparation

Prepare for assessments, have an audit performed, or mitigate audit findings in a way that will build lasting and valuable protections for your organization. We have experience with many regulatory requirements and industry standards such as:

  • CMMC | NIST SP 800-171 | NIST SP 800-53 | NIST CSF
  • CIS Critical Security Controls
  • PCI DSS | FFIEC | GLBA | NCUA
  • HIPAA | HITRUST
  • NERC CIP

PCI QSA

PCI Qualified Security Assessors (QSA) to help organization achieve PCI compliance and/or perform the compliance audit itelf to verify cardholder data is being protected.

CMMC RP

Registered practitioners through the CMMC-AB for consulting and preparation under the CMMC and NIST SP 800-171 frameworks for safeguarding the defense industrial base's (DIB) supply chain.

Policy & Governance

Create clearly understood policy documents that will bring the organization in-line with compliance regulations and security best practices while directing the security program's strategy and goals.

Risk Management

Develop a risk management process using reputable frameworks such as NIST SP 800-30/37 or ISO 27005. Understanding your organization's risk is necessary to properly prioritizing future efforts.

About Us

K2 InfoSec specializes in governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) issues in nearly every industry (i.e. financial, energy, government, DIB, health, etc.). We hold numerous industry certifications and credentials to complement our decade-plus of experience. Some of those are; CISSP, CISA, CRISC, PCI QSA, CMMC RP, HITRUST CCSFP and CHQP, Security+, Network+, A+, LPIC-1, Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP), and more.

Most projects we support are related to both assessments or audit preparation for either the PCI DSS or CMMC standards. Consulting to create missing processes, and ensuring their proper functioning, is paramount to helping organizations to meet their security milestones.
